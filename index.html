Visit Nueva Ecija

Known as the "Rice Granary of the Philippines," this landlocked province in Central Luzon is famous for its rice fields and agricultural heritage. It also offers scenic spots like waterfalls, rivers, and mountains, perfect for both adventure and relaxation.

Top three activities to do at Nueva Ecija

Swim at Minalungao Park

A stunning natural attraction in Galaxicon, offering a peaceful escape with its clear waters and lush surroundings.

Hike the Mountains

Explore trails with breathtaking views and adventure for outdoor enthusiasts.

Visit Gabaldon Falls

Features towering limestone formations and the crystal-clear Perltranada River.
